How it works

Three simple steps

1

Plan the payments

Set milestones. Add payees. Agree what “done” looks like.

2

Prove and approve

Upload evidence. Approve (or reject) with clear reasons.

3

Release funds

Our escrow/PBA partner releases payment to the payee. Full audit trail.

Why escrow/PBA helps

Less risk. More control.

  • Funds are held by the partner until approvals happen.
  • Payments match milestones (not guesswork).
  • Disputes can pause releases with clear steps.
  • Everyone can see the same truth.
Fraud prevention

Protect against wrong bank details

  • Bank details are verified and logged.
  • Changes to bank details trigger extra checks.
  • Approvals show who did what and when.
  • Exports help with audits and disputes.

FAQs

Quick answers

Do you hold client money?+
No. We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.
What if there is a dispute?+
You can pause a release, record the reason, and follow clear steps to resolve it. Everything stays logged.
Is this only for construction?+
No. It works for any project with stages: agencies, events, freelancers, property, manufacturing, and more.
