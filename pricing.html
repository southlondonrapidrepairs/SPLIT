Pricing

Simple pricing you can explain

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.

Choose the model that fits your project. We also keep partner costs transparent.

Option 1

Per project

Best for one-off builds or events.

  • One project workspace
  • Milestones + approvals
  • Audit trail + exports

Example: £[X] per project (based on size)

Option 2

Per month

Best for teams running projects every month.

  • Multiple active projects
  • Roles & permissions
  • Templates per industry

Example: £[X]/month

Option 3

Per transaction

Best when you want low fixed cost.

  • Low base fee
  • Small fee per release
  • Clear reporting

Partner fees

Clear note about escrow/PBA costs

The escrow/PBA partner may charge their own fees (for holding funds and making releases). We show these clearly during setup so there are no surprises.

  • Account setup fees (sometimes)
  • Release/transaction fees (sometimes)
  • Identity checks (sometimes)

Exact fees depend on the partner and the project setup.

VAT note: VAT depends on the project and each supplier’s VAT status. Savings vary. We charge VAT on our management fee where applicable. We don’t provide tax advice.
