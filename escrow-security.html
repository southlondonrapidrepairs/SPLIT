Security

Clear, safer project payments

Money handling: We do not hold client funds. Funds are held and released by our escrow/PBA partner.

This is not “send money and hope”. It is a simple approval system with a full record. Funds are held by a partner and released only after milestone approval.

Who does what

Simple roles

  • You: set milestones, review evidence, approve releases.
  • Payees: submit invoices and proof against milestones.
  • Escrow/PBA partner: holds funds and releases payments.
  • Our platform: workflow, approvals, audit trail, exports.
Controls

Approval before release

  • Milestones define what gets paid and when.
  • Evidence is attached (photos/docs).
  • Approvals are tracked (who, when, what).
  • Releases can be paused if needed.
Fraud prevention

Bank detail verification + change protection

  • Bank details are verified and logged at onboarding.
  • Bank detail changes trigger extra checks (and require approval).
  • All changes are recorded in the audit trail.

Tip: always confirm bank changes using a trusted contact method, not a new email address.

Audit trail

Keep proof for years

  • Export payments and approvals (CSV/PDF).
  • Keep evidence linked to each milestone.
  • Clear history if a dispute happens later.
VAT wording

Stay honest and safe

VAT depends on the project and each supplier’s VAT status. Savings vary. We charge VAT on our management fee where applicable. We don’t provide tax advice.
